One Summer Chicago

In June 2020, One Summer Chicago, in partnership with OPEN Center for the Arts, Chicago, brought together a cohort of youth and young adults ages 14 to 24 for a virtual internship. Our apprehensions gave way to dynamic and exciting stories. We came together to learn Storytelling and Urban Film. What we found was a community of fellow young artists in Chicago who wanted to share our stories.
